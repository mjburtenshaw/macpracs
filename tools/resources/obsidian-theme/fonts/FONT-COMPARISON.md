## Use Case Recommendations

### For Creative Writing Projects (Kuroki vault)

**Recommended:** **IBM Plex**

**Why:**
- Prose writing benefits from serif (Plex Serif)
- Worldbuilding notes benefit from systematic sans (Plex Sans)
- Still maintains the stark, archival aesthetic
- Won't fatigue you during 3-hour writing sessions
- Feels like "official documentation" not "literature"

### For Maximum Aesthetic Purity

**Recommended:** **Courier**

**Why:**
- Pure commitment to the field report aesthetic
- Everything is typewriter = everything is documentation
- Most aligned with AoT "Survey Corps logs" vibe
- Character alignment system works perfectly in monospace
- "Color is rare" = distinction is earned, not given through font choice

### For General Use / Readability First

**Recommended:** **Charter/Inter**

**Why:**
- Most readable for extended sessions
- Professional appearance
- Built-in fonts (Charter)
- Good for mixed content
- **But:** Doesn't align with Themodynamic philosophy
